About Karo Pitch

Built to give every Indian founder — wherever they are — a fair shot at funding.

What We Do

A structured pitch platform built for real founders

Karo Pitch is a curated investor pitch platform where early-stage founders from across India — especially from Tier-2, Tier-3 cities and Bharat — can present their businesses directly to investors in structured, high-quality pitch events.

Think of it as a closed-room investor event, made accessible. No gatekeepers. No geography bias. Just founders with real businesses getting in front of investors who are actively looking to deploy capital.

We exist because we've spent 5 years at KaroStartup hearing from thousands of founders who are building remarkable things — but have no pathway to investors. Karo Pitch is that pathway.
