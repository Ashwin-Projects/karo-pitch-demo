About Karo Pitch

What is Karo Pitch?

Karo Pitch is a platform designed to connect early-stage founders from across India with investors. Many founders in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities build strong businesses but struggle to access funding and visibility. Karo Pitch bridges this gap through curated pitch events and a startup discovery platform.
